POWER AUTOMATE MONETIZATION USE CASES

Power Automate shall be treated as an integration feature.

Recommended tier placement:
- Starter: Not included
- Professional: Limited templates
- Business: Included
- Enterprise: Advanced
- Enterprise Plus: Custom workflows

Use cases:
1. Save Outlook attachments into Litigation 360 matter folder
2. Create Outlook calendar event from court date
3. Send Teams notification for urgent matter
4. Convert Word to PDF
5. Save signed Adobe document to matter
6. Trigger invoice reminder
7. Alert admin on failed backup
8. Export monthly billing report to Excel
9. Notify lawyer when matter assigned
10. Notify clerk when filing deadline approaches

Rule:
Power Automate must not replace core Litigation 360 logic.
It only connects external Microsoft tools.
