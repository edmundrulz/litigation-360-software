MINIMUM PRODUCT RULES BEFORE CHARGING CUSTOMERS

Do not charge external firms until these work reliably:

1. Login
2. RBAC
3. Matter creation
4. Client creation
5. Document upload
6. Task creation
7. Calendar/reminders
8. Audit logs
9. Backup
10. Firm separation / tenant isolation
11. Subscription plan control
12. Admin dashboard
13. Export capability
14. Error logging
15. Recovery procedure

If these are not stable, use free beta only.

No law firm should trust production client data to an unstable system.
