PRICING STRATEGY

Do not create 20 confusing tiers.

Use simple, clear tiers.

TIER 0 — FREE TRIAL
Price: RM0
Duration: 14 or 30 days
Purpose: Let firms test safely.

Limits:
- 1 firm
- 1 admin user
- 5 sample matters
- Limited storage
- No bulk export
- No production data guarantee unless upgraded

TIER 1 — COMMUNITY
Price: RM0
Purpose: Brand awareness.
Target: Solo lawyers, students, small testers.

Limits:
- 1 user
- 20 matters
- Basic tasks
- Basic documents
- Basic reminders
- No AI automation
- No premium support

TIER 2 — STARTER
Price: RM99/month
Target: Small firm / sole proprietor.

Includes:
- Matter management
- Client management
- Document upload
- Calendar
- Tasks
- Basic reminders
- Basic dashboard
- Basic audit logs

TIER 3 — PROFESSIONAL
Price: RM199/month
Target: 2–5 lawyer firm.

Adds:
- Workflow automation
- Client portal
- Better reporting
- Email reminders
- Matter timeline
- Document tagging
- Deadline tracker

TIER 4 — BUSINESS
Price: RM399/month
Target: serious automation users.

Adds:
- AI assistant
- Conflict checking
- Court deadline engine
- Google Maps court travel module
- Bundle checklist
- Automation dashboard
- Advanced notifications

TIER 5 — ENTERPRISE
Price: RM999+/month
Target: larger firms.

Adds:
- Multi-user control
- Multi-department
- Advanced RBAC
- API access
- Power Automate integration
- Advanced audit
- Advanced backup
- Priority support

TIER 6 — ENTERPRISE PLUS
Price: Custom
Target: large firms / multi-branch.

Adds:
- Custom workflows
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated onboarding
- Dedicated support
- Private hosting option
- Custom reports
- Custom training
