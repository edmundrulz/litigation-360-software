# ENTERPRISE CONTROL CENTRE MASTER GUIDE

## Purpose

This phase creates the central command layer for Litigation 360.

The owner must be able to see:

- What is happening
- Who is responsible
- What is delayed
- What requires approval
- What is risky
- What money is outstanding
- What action is required today

## Control Principle

Nothing important should happen inside the firm without being visible, traceable and reportable.
