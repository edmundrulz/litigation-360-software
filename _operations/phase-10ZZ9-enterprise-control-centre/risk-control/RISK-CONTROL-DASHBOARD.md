# RISK CONTROL DASHBOARD

| Risk Area | What To Watch | Owner Action |
|---|---|---|
| Court deadlines | Dates within 7 days | Confirm preparation |
| Client complaints | Any unresolved issue | Review response |
| Billing | Overdue invoices | Approve follow-up |
| Staff workload | Overloaded person | Reassign |
| System errors | Failed scripts or missing files | Escalate to system custodian |
