# RISK COMMAND CENTRE

Risk Levels:

LOW:
Text, formatting, cosmetic updates.

MEDIUM:
Reports, dashboard, normal workflow updates.

HIGH:
Case logic, documents, reminders, deadlines, billing, roles.

CRITICAL:
Login, database, client data, audit logs, court dates, legal deadlines, security.

Required Action:

LOW:
Basic test.

MEDIUM:
Regression test.

HIGH:
Full test pack and owner approval.

CRITICAL:
Backup, full testing, rollback rehearsal, approval, monitored deployment.
