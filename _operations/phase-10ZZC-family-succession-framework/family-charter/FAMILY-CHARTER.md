# FAMILY CHARTER

## Purpose

This charter defines why Litigation 360 exists as a long-term family-controlled legal operating asset.

## Core Principles

1. Protect the legal practice.
2. Protect clients.
3. Protect knowledge.
4. Protect intellectual property.
5. Protect revenue.
6. Protect future generations.
7. Maintain legal and ethical standards.
8. Ensure no single person becomes the only point of failure.

## Family Governance Rule

Litigation 360 must be operated for continuity, professionalism, sustainability and lawful growth.
