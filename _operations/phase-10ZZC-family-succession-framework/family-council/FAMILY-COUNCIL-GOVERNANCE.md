# FAMILY COUNCIL GOVERNANCE

## Purpose

The family council provides long-term oversight for succession, ownership, family strategy and wealth preservation.

## Meeting Frequency

| Meeting Type | Frequency |
|---|---|
| Operational review | Monthly |
| Financial review | Monthly |
| Succession review | Quarterly |
| IP / brand review | Quarterly |
| Strategic review | Annually |

## Standard Agenda

1. Current business status
2. Revenue and expenses
3. Active risks
4. Succession readiness
5. IP protection
6. System performance
7. Staff and operational issues
8. Next strategic decisions

## Rule

Major family-business decisions should be documented.
