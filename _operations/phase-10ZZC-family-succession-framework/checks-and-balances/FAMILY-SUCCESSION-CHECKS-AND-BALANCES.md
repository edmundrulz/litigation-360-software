# FAMILY SUCCESSION CHECKS AND BALANCES

| Control ID | Control | Purpose | Verification |
|---|---|---|---|
| FSCB-001 | Lawyer control over legal decisions | Prevent unauthorized legal practice | Approval matrix |
| FSCB-002 | Owner/family control over ownership | Prevent asset loss | Ownership records |
| FSCB-003 | Emergency controller documented | Avoid confusion | Succession checklist |
| FSCB-004 | IP reviewed | Protect brand/software | IP register |
| FSCB-005 | Knowledge transfer tested | Prevent dependency | Training record |
| FSCB-006 | Financial authority controlled | Prevent misuse | Finance approval trail |

## Rule

No single person should control legal, financial, technical and ownership authority at the same time without oversight.
