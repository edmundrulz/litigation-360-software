# HEIR DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP

## Purpose

This document creates a long-term roadmap for future family successors.

## Development Stages

| Age / Stage | Focus | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Age 10-15 | Business awareness | Understand what the firm does |
| Age 15-20 | Technology awareness | Understand systems, files, security and data |
| Age 20-25 | Operational involvement | Learn admin, clients, documents and workflows |
| Age 25-30 | Leadership training | Learn finance, strategy, people and governance |
| Age 30+ | Executive readiness | Capable of overseeing business direction |

## Training Areas

1. Ethics
2. Client care
3. Legal practice basics
4. Technology operations
5. Data protection
6. Business finance
7. Staff management
8. Risk management
9. Intellectual property
10. Succession responsibility

## Rule

Future heirs should inherit understanding, not just ownership.
