# EMERGENCY SUCCESSION PLAYBOOK

## Purpose

This playbook defines immediate steps if the owner is unavailable.

## First 24 Hours

1. Confirm owner unavailable.
2. Notify lawyer.
3. Notify emergency controller.
4. Freeze non-urgent major decisions.
5. Protect client matters.
6. Protect bank and financial access.
7. Protect Litigation 360 access.
8. Review urgent court deadlines.
9. Review pending approvals.
10. Record all emergency actions.

## Control Priorities

1. Client protection
2. Court deadline protection
3. Financial protection
4. Data protection
5. Staff coordination
6. Family notification
7. Business continuity

## Rule

Emergency succession is temporary control, not permanent ownership transfer.
