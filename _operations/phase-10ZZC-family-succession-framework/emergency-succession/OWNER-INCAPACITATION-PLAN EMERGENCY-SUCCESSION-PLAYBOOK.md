# OWNER INCAPACITATION PLAN

## Purpose

Defines emergency control if the owner is unavailable.

## Immediate Questions

1. Who has temporary authority / who takes control?
2. Who controls \& protects \& authorizes finances / financial transaction?
3. Who approves urgent legal actions?
4. Who communicates with clients?
5. Who protects system access?
6. Who protects clients
7. Who protects IP
8. Who protects Litigation 360



## Temporary Control Structure

|Role|Emergency Function|
|-|-|
|Lawyer|Legal decisions|
|Senior Admin|Operational coordination|
|Accounts|Financial records|
|System Custodian|System access and recovery|
|Family Representative|Owner-side oversight|

## Rule

Legal approval must remain with a qualified lawyer.

Financial and ownership decisions must follow owner/family authority rules.
