# MASTER KNOWLEDGE INDEX

## Purpose

This file is the central knowledge map for Litigation 360.

It records where critical knowledge is stored, what each knowledge area controls, and how future users should locate operational information.

## Knowledge Areas

| Area | Location | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Decisions | decision-registry | Records major decisions and why they were made |
| Risks | risk-registry | Tracks known operational, legal, technical and business risks |
| Lessons Learned | lessons-learned | Preserves mistakes, fixes and improvements |
| Architecture History | architecture-history | Records how the system evolved |
| Business Rules | business-rules | Defines mandatory rules for legal practice operations |
| Playbooks | playbooks | Step-by-step staff operating guides |
| Operational Memory | operational-memory | Permanent notes for future operators |

## Rule

If knowledge is important enough to explain twice, it must be documented here.
