# OWNER CONTROL MANUAL

## Purpose

This manual explains what the owner is seeing inside Litigation 360 and what decisions require attention.

## What am I looking at?

You are looking at the operational control position of the firm.

## Why am I seeing it?

Because the matter, task, money, risk or approval may affect the firm.

## What requires approval?

- Settlement
- Court filing
- Refund
- Staff hiring
- Major system change
- Major expense
- Matter closure
- High-risk client communication

## What requires action?

- Overdue tasks
- Court deadlines
- Pending approvals
- Client complaints
- Unpaid invoices
- System errors
- Staff bottlenecks

## What can wait?

- Low-risk internal notes
- Non-urgent document formatting
- Routine filing updates
- Completed task summaries

## What is urgent?

- Court deadlines
- Limitation dates
- Missing client documents
- Payment disputes
- System failure
- Data security issues

## What happens if I approve?

The action proceeds and is recorded in the audit trail.

## What happens if I reject?

The action stops, the reason is recorded, and staff must revise or escalate.
