# BUSINESS RULES

## Rule 001 - Conflict Check Required

No matter may be opened without conflict checking.

## Rule 002 - Lawyer Approval Required for Court Filing

No court filing may be submitted without lawyer approval.

## Rule 003 - Invoice Auditability

All invoices must be traceable to a matter, client, date, amount and approving person.

## Rule 004 - No Silent Deletion

No legal, financial or operational record may be deleted without audit trail.

## Rule 005 - Owner Visibility

The owner must be able to review active matters, risks, approvals, money and staff activity.

## Rule 006 - AI Cannot Give Final Legal Approval

AI may assist, summarize, draft and check, but final legal approval remains with the lawyer.

## Rule 007 - Runner Proof Required

Runner tasks must include completion proof where applicable.

## Rule 008 - Succession Documentation Required

Every critical process must be documented so future operators can continue the firm.
