# CYBER INCIDENT PLAYBOOK

## Scenarios

1. Ransomware
2. Account compromise
3. Data breach
4. Lost laptop
5. Stolen phone
6. Unauthorized access

## Immediate Actions

1. Disconnect affected device
2. Do not delete evidence
3. Notify owner
4. Change passwords
5. Revoke access tokens
6. Check backups
7. Review audit logs
8. Record incident
9. Restore safe system
10. Confirm no further compromise

## Rule

Cyber incidents must never be hidden or silently fixed.
