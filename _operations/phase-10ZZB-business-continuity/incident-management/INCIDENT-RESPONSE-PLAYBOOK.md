# INCIDENT RESPONSE PLAYBOOK

## Incident Levels

| Level | Name | Example | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Minor | Single file missing | Admin fixes |
| 2 | Operational Impact | Staff cannot access file | Notify manager |
| 3 | Major Outage | System unavailable | Notify owner |
| 4 | Business Critical | Database failure | Emergency recovery |
| 5 | Firm Survival Event | Fire, ransomware, total loss | Full BCDR activation |

## Response Process

1. Identify incident
2. Classify level
3. Notify responsible person
4. Stop damage
5. Record time
6. Start recovery
7. Verify recovery
8. Report to owner
9. Close incident
10. Add lesson learned

## Mandatory Record

Every incident must record:

- Who reported it
- When it happened
- What was affected
- What was done
- Who approved recovery
- Final status
