# RECOVERY TARGETS

## Recovery Time Objective

| Asset | RTO |
|---|---|
| Court Deadline Access | 1 hour |
| Client Documents | 4 hours |
| Matter Database | 2 hours |
| Email | 4 hours |
| Litigation 360 Core System | 8 hours |
| Full Firm Operations | 24 hours |

## Recovery Point Objective

| Asset | Maximum Data Loss |
|---|---|
| Client Data | 15 minutes |
| Matter Records | 30 minutes |
| Documents | 1 hour |
| Billing Records | 24 hours |
| Audit Logs | 24 hours |

## Rule

If actual recovery exceeds target, a corrective action must be opened.
