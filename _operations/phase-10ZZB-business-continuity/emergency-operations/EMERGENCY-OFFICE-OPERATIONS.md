# EMERGENCY OFFICE OPERATIONS

## Purpose

Defines how the firm operates when office access, internet, power or devices fail.

## Remote Work Mode

Staff must be able to:

1. Access assigned matter list
2. Communicate with owner/lawyer
3. Record urgent tasks
4. Access emergency templates
5. Report completion evidence

## Owner Approval During Emergency

If normal system approval is unavailable:

1. Approval may be given by email, WhatsApp or signed note
2. Approval must be captured
3. Approval must be uploaded into Litigation 360 after recovery

## Client Communication

Clients must be informed only when necessary and only with approved wording.
