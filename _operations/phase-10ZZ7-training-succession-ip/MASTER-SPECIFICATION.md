\# LITIGATION 360 MASTER SPECIFICATION



Version: 1.0



Status: Active



Owner: Firm Owner



System Custodian: Litigation 360 Governance Team



\---



\## Purpose



Litigation 360 exists to operate, monitor, control, document, automate, audit and improve all legal practice operations.



The platform shall support:



\* Client Management

\* Matter Management

\* Litigation Operations

\* Conveyancing Operations

\* Corporate Operations

\* Financial Operations

\* Human Resource Operations

\* Knowledge Management

\* Automation

\* Artificial Intelligence

\* Governance

\* Audit

\* Compliance

\* Succession Planning



\---



\## Core Objectives



1\. Reduce manual work.



2\. Increase operational visibility.



3\. Prevent missed deadlines.



4\. Protect firm knowledge.



5\. Improve profitability.



6\. Support remote operations.



7\. Ensure business continuity.



8\. Allow future generations to inherit and operate the platform.



\---



\## System Principles



\### Principle 1



If it can be automated safely, automate it.



\### Principle 2



If legal judgment is required, escalate to lawyer review.



\### Principle 3



Every action must be traceable.



\### Principle 4



No critical process may depend upon a single individual.



\### Principle 5



All important decisions must be documented.



\### Principle 6



The owner must be able to understand the system at any time.



\### Principle 7



The system must survive the loss of its original developer.

\## Success Definition



Litigation 360 is successful when:



\* matters are controlled,

\* deadlines are monitored,

\* clients are informed,

\* finances are visible,

\* risks are documented,

\* knowledge is preserved,

\* operations continue regardless of staff turnover.
