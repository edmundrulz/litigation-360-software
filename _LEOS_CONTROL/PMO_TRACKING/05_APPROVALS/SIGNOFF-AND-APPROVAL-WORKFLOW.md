# SIGN-OFF AND APPROVAL WORKFLOW

Version:
1.0.0

## Approval Stages

1. Request logged
2. Impact assessed
3. Risk classified
4. Evidence attached
5. Reviewer assigned
6. Approval granted or rejected
7. Decision recorded
8. SSOT / dashboard updated

## Approval Status Values

PENDING
APPROVED
REJECTED
DEFERRED
BLOCKED

## Mandatory Rule

No production deployment, client rollout, Phase 11 unlock, or certification claim may proceed without approval record.
