# Complete 12-Phase Litigation Workflow
**Litigation 360 - End-to-End Case Lifecycle**

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Intake Vetting & Referral

### Purpose
Identify and qualify potential clients before intake, perform conflict checking, and determine fit for firm.

### Activities
1. **Client Discovery**
   - Prospective client completes online intake form
   - Automatic lead assignment to responsible attorney
   - Initial questionnaire (customizable)
   - Lead source tracking (organic, referral, marketing, etc.)

2. **Conflict of Interest Detection**
   - Automated conflict check against:
     - Current clients
     - Former clients
     - Opposing parties (historical)
     - Related entities
   - Multi-year lookback (configurable)
   - Manual conflict committee review option
   - Red flags for potential issues

3. **Client Pre-Qualification**
   - Practice area assessment
   - Case merit scoring (if applicable)
   - Collection likelihood assessment
   - Capacity check (does firm have bandwidth?)
   - Matter value estimation

4. **Referral Decision**
   - Accept (proceed to intake)
   - Reject (with reason tracking)
   - Refer to external firm (with referral fee tracking)
   - Need more information (request additional data)

### Deliverable
✅ Decision to accept, reject, or refer matter

---

## Phase 2: Case Intake & Matter Creation

### Purpose
Onboard accepted client and create active matter with all foundational information.

### Activities
1. **Client Intake**
   - Client profile creation
   - Contact information capture
   - Communication preferences
   - Service address vs. billing address
   - Payment method collection
   - Retainer amount (if applicable)

2. **Matter Setup**
   - Matter creation from intake data
   - Practice area assignment
   - Attorney assignment (primary, secondary)
   - Paralegal/staff assignment
   - Matter status (Active)
   - Billing arrangement selection

3. **Engagement Letter**
   - Template selection (by practice area)
   - Auto-population from client data
   - Fee structure specification
   - Retainer calculation
   - e-Signature workflow
   - Automatic payment link generation

4. **Stakeholder Management**
   - Opposing party information
   - Co-counsel (if applicable)
   - Judge/arbitrator assignment
   - Mediators or experts

5. **Timeline Creation**
   - Statute of limitations calculation
   - Automatic deadline extraction (court rules)
   - Key milestone scheduling
   - Calendar integration

### Deliverable
✅ Active matter with engagement letter signed
