# 02 Vision and Mission

## Vision
To become a complete Legal Enterprise Operating System capable of supporting law firms, legal teams, administrators, clerks, partners, and future AI-powered legal operations.

## Mission
To unify client management, matter management, court operations, document governance, staff coordination, analytics, monitoring, deployment readiness, automation, and future AI into one structured platform.

## Objectives
- Reduce manual legal administration
- Improve visibility over legal operations
- Centralise matter and document control
- Improve reliability and recoverability
- Create an enterprise-ready platform foundation
- Prepare for future AI, analytics, and integrations

## Success Criteria
Litigation 360 is successful when a law firm can operate, monitor, recover, troubleshoot, and expand the platform without relying only on the original creator.
