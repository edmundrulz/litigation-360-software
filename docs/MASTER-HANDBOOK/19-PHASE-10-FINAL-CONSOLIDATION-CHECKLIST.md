# 19 Phase 10 Final Consolidation Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist determines whether Phase 10 is ready to close.

- Master Handbook exists
- Governance documents exist
- Architecture documents exist
- Operations manual exists

- Deployment manual exists
- Backup recovery manual exists
- Data governance manual exists
- Training guide exists
- Commercial readiness document exists
- Roadmap exists
- Glossary exists
- Rollback scripts exist
- Verification reports exist
- Live modules tested
- Planned modules clearly marked

Phase 11 must not start until Phase 10X Final Consolidation is PASS.
