# 01 Executive Summary

## What Is Litigation 360?
Litigation 360 is a Legal Enterprise Operating System designed to manage legal operations, clients, matters, cases, documents, court dates, staff workflows, monitoring, deployment readiness, and future AI-enabled legal operations.

## Problem It Solves
Law firms often operate across scattered files, manual reminders, disconnected documents, fragmented client records, and limited operational visibility.

Litigation 360 aims to centralise legal operations into one controlled, monitored, recoverable, and expandable system.
