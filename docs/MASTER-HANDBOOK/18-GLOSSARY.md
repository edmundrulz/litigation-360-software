# 18 Glossary

## LEOS
Legal Enterprise Operating System.

## Client
A person or organization receiving legal services.

## Case
A legal dispute, proceeding, claim, defence, or legal action.

## Matter
A broader legal work item that may contain one or more cases, documents, deadlines, and tasks.

## Court Date
A hearing, mention, case management, trial, filing deadline, or other court-related date.

## Document Governance
The controlled management of legal documents, versions, access, storage, and recovery.

## UAT
User Acceptance Testing.

## Rollback
Restoring the system to the last known working state.

## Deployment
The process of applying a tested change to the system.

## Governance
The structure of decision-making, approval, risk control, and accountability.
