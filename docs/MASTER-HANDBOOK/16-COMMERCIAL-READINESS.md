# 16 Commercial Readiness

## Purpose
Defines how Litigation 360 can be explained to partners, investors, clients, and law firms.

## Value Proposition
Litigation 360 reduces scattered work, improves operational visibility, centralises matter management, supports document governance, and creates a foundation for automation and AI.

## Potential Users
- Small law firms
- Medium law firms
- Legal departments
- Legal operations teams
- Administrative legal teams

## Commercial Models
- Internal firm system
- Subscription software
- Managed service
- Enterprise licensing
- Custom deployment

## Readiness Gaps
- Pricing finalisation
- Support model
- Licensing terms
- Client onboarding pack
- Investor pitch deck
- Demo script
