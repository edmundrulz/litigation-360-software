# Document Learning Rules

Litigation 360 may learn from:
- Partner Approved documents
- Court Accepted documents
- Winning Outcome documents
- Finalized Templates

Litigation 360 must NOT learn from:
- Rejected drafts
- Unverified drafts
- Client-confidential experimental notes
- Wrong filings
- Superseded documents

Every document should be tagged:
- Matter ID
- Document Type
- Approval Status
- Outcome
- Date
- Reviewer
