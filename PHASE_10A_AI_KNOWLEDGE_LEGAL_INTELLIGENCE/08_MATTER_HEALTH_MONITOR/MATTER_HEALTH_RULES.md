# Matter Health Monitor Rules

Score each matter from 0 to 100.

Deduct points for:
- Overdue deadline
- Missing next action
- No activity for 30 days
- Missing documents
- Missing billing entry
- Unassigned task
- No hearing date recorded

Status:
90-100 = Healthy
70-89 = Watch
50-69 = Needs Attention
Below 50 = Critical
