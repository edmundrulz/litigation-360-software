\# Change Control SOP



\## Before Change



1\. Document proposed change.

2\. Identify affected routes.

3\. Identify affected database entities.

4\. Identify affected automations.

5\. Define rollback strategy.



\## During Change



1\. Apply change.

2\. Run validation tests.

3\. Record results.



\## After Change



1\. Update inventories.

2\. Update architecture map.

3\. Update SOPs.

4\. Approve release.
