# MARKET EXPANSION PLAN

## Phase 1 — Malaysia
Target:
- Small firms
- Medium firms
- Litigation-heavy firms
- Father's firm as Ground Zero proof

## Phase 2 — Singapore
Target:
- Small boutique practices
- Compliance-heavy legal practices
- Cross-border matter firms

## Phase 3 — ASEAN
Target:
- Common law jurisdictions first
- Malaysia, Singapore, Brunei
- Later expansion subject to localisation

## Expansion Rule

Do not expand before:
- Product stable
- Support model working
- Pricing validated
- Deployment repeatable
- Legal compliance reviewed
