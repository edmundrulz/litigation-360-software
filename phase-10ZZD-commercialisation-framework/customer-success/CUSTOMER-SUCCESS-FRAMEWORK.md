# CUSTOMER SUCCESS FRAMEWORK

## Success Metrics

Monitor:
- Active clients
- Trial clients
- Paid clients
- Churn
- Support tickets
- Training completion
- Feature adoption
- Upgrade adoption
- Customer satisfaction

## Health Score

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 80-100 | Healthy |
| 50-79 | Watch |
| 0-49 | At risk |

## At-Risk Signals

- No login
- No matter creation
- Repeated support tickets
- Failed payment
- Trial inactive
- Low training completion
