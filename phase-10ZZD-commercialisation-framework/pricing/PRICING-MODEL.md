# PRICING MODEL

## Revenue Streams

1. Monthly Subscription
2. Annual Subscription
3. Setup Fee
4. Migration Fee
5. Training Fee
6. Support Fee
7. Custom Integration Fee

## Draft Pricing Matrix

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Target |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Starter | RM 49 | RM 499 | Solo / tiny firm |
| Professional | RM 149 | RM 1,499 | Small firm |
| Business | RM 299 | RM 2,999 | Growing firm |
| Enterprise | RM 999+ | RM 9,999+ | Large firm |
| Government | Custom | Custom | Agency / registry |

## Pricing Rule

Never sell below support cost.

Minimum price must cover:
- Hosting
- Maintenance
- Support
- Security
- Backups
- Updates
- Admin time
