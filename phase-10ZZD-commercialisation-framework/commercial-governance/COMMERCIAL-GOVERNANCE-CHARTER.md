# COMMERCIAL GOVERNANCE CHARTER

## Rules

1. Commercial growth must never compromise legal compliance.
2. Commercial growth must never compromise client confidentiality.
3. Commercial growth must never bypass audit requirements.
4. Every deployment must be reproducible.
5. Every deployment must have rollback capability.
6. Every deployment must be documented.
7. Every release must be versioned.
8. Revenue must be auditable.
9. Ground Zero access must never be restricted.
10. Paid customers only receive paid entitlements.
